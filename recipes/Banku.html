Banku

Home

Description

Banku is a traditional Ghanaian dish made from a fermented mixture of corn and cassava dough.

It is typically cooked by stirring the dough mixture in hot water until it becomes smooth and stretchy. Banku is commonly enjoyed with soups or stews, especially okra soup or grilled tilapia with pepper sauce.

Ingredients

  • Corn Dough
  • Cassava Dough
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix the Doughs
  2. Add Salt
  3. Start Cooking
  4. Stir Continuously
