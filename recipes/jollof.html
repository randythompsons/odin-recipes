Description

Jollof rice is a popular West African dish made with rice cooked in a flavorful tomato-based sauce.

It typically includes ingredients like tomatoes, onions, peppers, garlic, and a blend of local spices.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Blend the Base
  2. Prepare the Sauce
  3. Season the Sauce
  4. Add Rice
