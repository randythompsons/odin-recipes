Description

Yam is a starchy tuber commonly eaten in many parts of Africa, the Caribbean, and Asia.

It has a rough, brown skin and white, yellow, or purple flesh depending on the variety.

Ingredients

  • Yam tuber
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Peel the Yam
  2. Cut into Pieces
  3. Rinse Again
  4. Boil the Yam
